About Us

Our Company

Established in 2009, Yiwu JIHONG FASHION Co., Ltd. is strategically nestled in the heart of Yiwu, China's epicenter for small commodities. Our robust infrastructure, backed by a registered capital of 5 million RMB, stretches across 7,755.31 square meters, with a sophisticated built-up facility encompassing 29,244.75 square meters, set within the esteemed Su Xi High-Tech Industrial Park.

At JIHONG FASHION, we are more than just a fashion enterprise; we are a symphony of design innovation, meticulous production, and dynamic sales. Our unparalleled production teams, steadfast commitment to quality, and an enduring promise of exceptional service have ushered our products onto the global stage. Driven by a vision to elevate everyday living through quality fashion, Ji Hong Fashion has become synonymous with intricate designs, select materials, and unmatched craftsmanship. As we challenge traditional boundaries and seek mutual growth, our ethos revolves around fostering partnerships that champion innovation and shared success.

As we look to the future, our focus is clear: to lead with transformative management practices, accelerate our technological endeavors, nurture talent, and fortify our collaborative ties with top-tier academic and research institutions. Emphasizing brand fortification and the sanctity of intellectual property rights, we march confidently towards global prominence.
